Our Story

From Street Food to Your Table

Founded in 1970, Kottu Paradise Restaurant began as a humble street food stall in the heart of Colombo. Our founder, inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Sri Lanka, set out to perfect the art of kottu making.

Today, we continue to honor those traditions while bringing innovative twists to this beloved street food. Every chop, every spice, and every serving carries the same passion that started our journey.

Our Mission

At Kottu Paradise, our mission is to deliver the authentic flavors of Sri Lankan kottu with a modern twist, offering a variety of delicious, fresh, and high-quality meals that cater to all taste buds. We aim to create a welcoming and vibrant dining experience, where every customer feels the warmth of our hospitality and the passion in our cooking.
